Each player starts with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end. To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn. If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand.

Disclaimer: The 'Bet Amount' on this website uses fake money. No payouts will be awarded so there will be no "cash outs." This game is free to play for everyone strictly for fun.
